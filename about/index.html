noRth is aimed at intermediate R users and consists of a half-day (optional) short course and a day of interactive talks. The conference will be held on August 15-16, 2019 in Bruininks Hall on the campus of the University of Minnesota. The tentative schedule is below (i.e. subject to changes).

Thursday, August 15, 2019

1:00 - 1:25 pm Workshop Registration
1:30 - 2:45 pm Shiny Workshop Pt. I
2:45 - 3:00 pm Coffee Break & Networking
3:00 - 4:30 pm Shiny Workshop Pt. II
4:30 - ? Happy Hour at TBD

Friday, August 16, 2019

8:00 - 8:45 am noRth Registration with coffee and light breakfast
8:50 - 9:05 am Welcome
9:05 - 10:05 am Plenary 1
10:05 - 10:30 am Coffee Break & Networking
10:35 - 11:10 am Breakout 1a Breakout 1b
11:20 - 11:55 am Breakout 2a Breakout 2b
12:00 - 1:20 pm Lunch Break
1:30 - 2:30 pm Plenary 2
2:30 - 2:55 pm Coffee Break/Networking
3:00 - 3:35 pm Breakout 3a Breakout 3b
3:45 - 4:20 pm Breakout 4a Breakout 4b
4:30 - 4:40 pm Closing
4:40 - ? Happy Hour at TBD

noRth is a collaboration between the following groups:

  • R-Ladies Twin Cities
  • Twin Cities R User Group
  • Institute for Research and Statistical Applications
